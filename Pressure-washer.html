Pressure washer

Pressure washing is the process of cleaning surfaces with high pressure water sprays, which has the benefit of providing a better result and in turn helping the environment since the use of chemical and toxic materials is significantly reduced.

Our pressure washing services are highly effective for:

  • Extraction of grease, rubber, stains, sand
  • Strip paint
  • Deep disinfection
  • Elimination of mold formation
  • Elimination of chemical residues and other contaminants
  • Creation of a clean, healthy and safe environment.
