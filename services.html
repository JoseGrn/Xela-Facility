Services

Restaurants services

The cleanliness of a restaurant is extremely important, it allows food, facilities, machinery and utensils to be kept in good condition, avoiding poisoning due to cross contamination and thus ensuring optimal hygienic conditions for customers.

Office services

The office is the letter of introduction of a professional, which is why at Xela Facility Services we have highly trained personnel to provide you with quality service and leave each of your environments clean, disinfected and safe for use.

Construction services

At Xela Facility Services we have extensive experience in the general cleaning of your buildings, thanks to our highest quality, eco-friendly products and our highly trained staff to leave the cleaning of your different types of projects in perfect condition for a new use, rent or sale.

Gym Services

The healthiness of both the facilities and elements as well as the environment is essential for the correct development of the activities carried out in the gyms.

High Dusting services

This service consists of the process of cleaning hard-to-reach places, since the usual cleaning services often overlook these spaces because they are difficult to reach.

Pressure Washer Services

Pressure washing is the process of cleaning surfaces with high pressure water sprays, which has the benefit of providing a better result and in turn helping the environment since the use of chemical and toxic materials is significantly reduced.
